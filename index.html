Bowen Jing

GitHub | Google Scholar
I am a 5th year Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at MIT, co-advised by Tommi Jaakkola and Bonnie Berger. I work on generative models for drug discovery, molecular simulation, and protein design.

Previously, I completed a B.S. in Computer Science at Stanford University. I was advised by Ron Dror and worked on equivariant neural networks for protein structure.
